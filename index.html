Maple OreCollector

A random number is generated at the start of the game. There are four gems on the right (bronze/gold/mithril/silver).

By clicking on the gems, you get a specific amount of points (displayed on the counter).

You must match the points (counter) to the random number to win. You will lose if your points (counter) is higher than the random number.

Gems will not show you the value until you click on them.

The game will restart with new numbers after one wins or loses.

(Bonus: Win 3x for surprise)
